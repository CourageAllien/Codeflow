# ColdFlow Command Variations

This document lists all natural language variations you can use for each command type.

## Overall Campaign Stats

**Purpose:** View total email sent and all-time performance metrics since contract start.

### Command Variations:

**Basic Queries:**
- "Overall campaign stats"
- "Overall campaign stats for Adaline"
- "Show overall stats for RocketReach"
- "Total campaign performance for Privy"

**Total Email Sent:**
- "Total emails sent for Adaline"
- "How many emails sent for RocketReach since contract start?"
- "Total email sent since contract began"
- "All time email sent for this client"
- "Show me total emails sent for Privy"

**Performance Metrics:**
- "Overall performance for Adaline"
- "All time stats for RocketReach"
- "Campaign stats since contract start"
- "Give me overall metrics for Privy"
- "Show me total campaign statistics"

**With Client Name:**
- "Overall stats for [Client Name]"
- "Total emails for [Client Name]"
- "All time performance for [Client Name]"

### Command Variations:

- "Number of meetings booked"

**For Specific Client:**
- "How many meetings booked for Privy?"
- "Meetings booked for Adaline"
- "Count meetings for RocketReach"

## ACTL & Booked Meeting Tracker

**Purpose:** Generate comprehensive dashboard with all clients' performance metrics.

### Command Variations:

**Standard Format:**
- "Give me ACTL & Booked Meeting Tracker for December 5 2024"
- "ACTL & Booked Meeting Tracker for January 15 2025"
- "Show ACTL tracker for November 20 2024"

**Variations:**
- "Give me the tracker for December 5 2024"
- "ACTL tracker for January 15 2025"
- "Booked Meeting Tracker for December 5 2024"
- "Show me ACTL for December 5 2024"

**Date Formats:**
- "ACTL tracker for December 5 2024" (month day year)
- "ACTL tracker for Dec 5 2024" (abbreviated month)
- "ACTL tracker for 12/5/2024" (numeric date)
- "ACTL tracker for today" (current date)

---

## Tips for Best Results

1. **Be Specific:** Include client name or campaign name when you know it
2. **Natural Language:** Just describe what you want - no need for exact syntax
3. **Follow-up Questions:** After getting a count, ask "who" or "show details" for more info
4. **Date Format:** For ACTL tracker, any date format works (December 5 2024, Dec 5 2024, 12/5/2024)

## Example Workflows

```
> Overall campaign stats for Privy

```

**Workflow 2: Check Meetings**
